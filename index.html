Who Are We?

Heart of Generation Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities across Kenya. We focus on education, nutrition, health, and empowerment programs that transform lives and give hope to children, youth, and families in underserved areas.

With every project, every meal, and every act of compassion, we are building a generation that is stronger, healthier, and full of opportunity.
