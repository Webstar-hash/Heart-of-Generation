About Us

Heart of Generation is a community based organization that provides food, training and scholarships to those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. We believe in building a sustainable future where every child and adult can thrive with dignity and hope.

Our Vision

To create a world where compassion drives progress and every generation is equipped with the tools, knowledge, and support to build a better tomorrow.

Our Story

Heart of Generation Foundation was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate individuals who saw the need to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity. What began as a small local effort has grown into a nationwide movement dedicated to nurturing hope and inspiring change through youth empowerment, education, and social programs.
